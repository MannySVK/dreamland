Long time ago, our ancestors invented a strong source of the power which they chained and locked into a small piece of stone by drawing an ornament on it, the Talisman.
Talisman is providing the owner mysterious effect, but the power is two-edged. It is improving you one ability but at the same time it is giving you handicap in other abilities,
Because of this side-effect the usage of talismans was pronounced as dangerous and all talismans where destroyed, but I am slowly recovering this ancient knowledge.
If you bring me neccessary materials, I am able to create Talismans for you. The quality is not perfect, but when I get more knowledge of them, I will be able to improve your Talismans to reduce negative side-effects.
