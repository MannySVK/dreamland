In the whole world it is possible to obtain Essences. They are special crystals filled with power, which represents basic elements in the nature. Therefore Fire, Water, Wind, Earth, Dark and Holy Essence can be found.
Essences very precious material with high power and they are rare to find in large amount. They can be used mostly in high quality item creation or item enhancement.
If you bring me some Essences, I am able to exchange them for you. But keep in mind, I can only exchange similar Essences, like Fire to Water or Water to Fire. I case you don't need them, I can also buy them from you.
